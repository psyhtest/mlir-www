Specialization for linalg::GenericOp and linalg::IndexedGenericOp.

A pattern to convert Generic Linalg operations which work on tensors to use buffers. BufferPlacement pass should be later used to move Alloc operations to the correct positions and insert the missing Dealloc operations in the correct places.
